Conversion pipeline

Cart interest does not guarantee purchase follow-through

Even when an item is added to the cart, the follow-through conversion rate can remain low for that product. These gaps show the store needs stronger incentives, clearer checkout paths, and timely reminders to help customers continue through the conversion pipeline.

Recommendations

Recommendations for the Google Merchandise Store

Use acquisition, location, device, and product behavior findings to create targeted promotions that improve conversion while protecting channel value.

Direct-site discounts

Offer weekly and monthly user product discounts for shoppers who visit the site directly.

Affiliate-link discounts

Offer smaller discounts for affiliate-link shoppers to preserve affiliate attribution while encouraging conversion.

City and country promotions

Tailor promotions by city and country, prioritizing high-user markets such as New York, Toronto, Mountain View, and the United States.

Device-specific campaigns

Build Android and mobile promotions because Android and mobile have the highest user volume.

Product-focused merchandising

Promote high-purchase items such as the G25gle Birthday Mug and G25gle Birthday Tee while improving conversion paths for high-revenue items such as the Super G Timbuk2 Recycled Backpack.
